About Jane Eisner Art

Founded in 2010, Jane Eisner Art emerged from a lifelong passion for creating beauty through visual expression. What began as a personal journey in a small studio has blossomed into a renowned gallery representing the intersection of traditional techniques and contemporary vision.

Our mission is to create meaningful connections through art, bringing unique perspectives and emotional depth to every piece we create. We believe in the power of original artwork to transform spaces and touch lives, maintaining the highest standards of artistic integrity while embracing innovation and personal expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the commission process work?

We begin with a consultation to discuss your vision, style preferences, and space requirements. After agreeing on the concept and details, we provide a timeline and begin creation with regular updates throughout the process.

What are your shipping policies?

We offer worldwide shipping with full insurance and tracking. All artwork is professionally packed to ensure safe delivery. Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days, while international shipping may take 7-14 days.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your artwork, we offer a 30-day return policy for original pieces, subject to our return guidelines and condition requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For commissioned works, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due upon completion.

How do you handle artwork authentication?

Each piece comes with a certificate of authenticity, detailed documentation of the artwork's creation, and our gallery seal. We maintain comprehensive records of all artwork for future reference.
